Domain: E-Commerce

Client Requirement: We want to build a web-based online shopping application where customers can easily browse our products, view available offers, and place orders with a smooth checkout process. The application should provide a simple and intuitive user experience, with essential features such as a product catalog, shopping cart, and secure payment options. Our main goal is to create a reliable platform that makes online purchasing convenient for our customers while allowing us to manage products, offers, and orders efficiently from the backend.

Scope Consideration:
1. Product Catalog Management – Add, update, and view products with basic details (name, price, stock).
2. Offers & Promotions – Showcase discounts and special deals to attract buyers.
3. Shopping Cart & Checkout – Add/remove products, view totals, and confirm an order.
4. Order Tracking – Customers can check their order status (Processing, Shipped, Delivered).

Future Roadmap (excluded from this workshop):
1. AI-driven product recommendations.
2. Personalized offers & loyalty programs.
3. Real-time stock updates from suppliers.
4. Integration with logistics/marketing platforms.
5. Subscription-based premium features.

Workshop Goal - Create a simple and user-friendly e-commerce platform where customers can browse products, view offers, and place orders, with scalability to support future advanced features.
